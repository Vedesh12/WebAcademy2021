My Personal Values/Rules

Values have significant power to unite and energise people. Shared values can connect, where race, religion, politics and gender can divide. Becoming a values-driven leader means understanding what is important to you personally and collectively and being committed to acting on those values.Personal values are things that you keep close to yourself that intensifies your life for the best. These rules/values can be shared with others to make thier lives better. The rules I follow are to stick up for my self, get good grades and to have fun.
